About Us

The Egyptian Rheumatology & Musculoskeletal Ultrasound Foundation (ERMUF) is a non-profit corporation that was founded to deliver continuous medical education in rheumatology and offer updated knowledge and skill in musculoskeletal ultrasound to enrich rheumatology practice with pivotal non-invasive tool in diagnosis, assessment of disease activity, as well as monitoring treatment plans for our rheumatology patients. We intend to give utmost support to young rheumatologists providing  updated knowledge in rheumatology from expertise in the field, help them develop skill in musculoskeletal ultrasound from scratch and promote this skill by serial courses and workshops. A third line that is crucial to our establish ment are research teams running research in different rheumatology fields as well as musculoskeletal ultrasound
